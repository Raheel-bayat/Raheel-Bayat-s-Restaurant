Biriani


Ingredients
  • Chicken: 800 grams, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • Basmati Rice: 2 cups, soaked for 30 minutes
  • Yogurt: 2 tablespoons
  • Onions: 2 large, thinly sliced
  • Tomatoes: 1 cup, chopped
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 3 teaspoons
  • Spices: Turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, cumin seeds, bay leaves, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves
  • Mint and Coriander Leaves: Fresh, for garnish
  • Saffron Milk: Optional, for aroma and color
  • Oil or Ghee: For cooking
Instructions
  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Coat the chicken pieces and let them marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Prepare the Rice: Boil water with bay leaves, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and salt. Cook the soaked rice until 70% done, then drain.
  3. Cook the Chicken: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Fry onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste, and the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is tender and the oil separates.
  4. Layering: In a large pot, layer the partially cooked rice and chicken alternately. Sprinkle mint and coriander leaves, fried onions, and saffron milk between layers.
  5. Dum Cooking: Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Rest and Serve: Let the biriyani rest for 10 minutes before serving. Pair it with a simple salad.



Kabuli Pulao


Ingredients
  • Rice: 2 cups, soaked for 30 minutes
  • Meat: 500 grams
  • Onions: 2 large, thinly sliced
  • Carrots: 2
  • Raisins: 1/2 cup
  • Almonds and Pistachios: 1/4 cup each, slivered
  • Spices: Cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and black pepper
  • Salt: To taste
  • Sugar: 1 tablespoon
  • Oil or Ghee: For cooking
  • Saffron Milk: Optional, for aroma and color
Instructions
  1. Cook the Meat: In a pot, heat oil or ghee and sauté onions until golden brown. Add the meat, salt, and spices. Cook until the meat is tender. Remove the meat and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Rice: Boil water with salt and whole spices (cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves). Cook the soaked rice until 70% done, then drain.
  3. Make Carrots and Raisins: In the same pot, add oil and sauté julienned carrots with sugar until caramelized. Add raisins and cook until plump. Remove and set aside.
  4. Layering: In a large pot, layer the rice, cooked meat, and the carrot-raisin mixture. Sprinkle slivered almonds and pistachios between layers.
  5. Dum Cooking: Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve: Garnish with additional nuts and serve hot with yogurt or salad.

Beef Burger


Ingredients
  • Ground Beef: 500 grams (preferably with some fat for juiciness)
  • Onion: 1 small, finely chopped
  • Egg: 1
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Burger bread
  • Vegetable Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Optional Toppings: Lettuce, tomato slices, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise


    • Instructions
    1. Prepare the Patties: In a bowl, mix the ground beef, chopped onion, egg, salt, and pepper. Divide the mixture into four equal portions and shape them into patties about 2 cm thick.
    2. Cook the Patties: Heat a grill or frying pan over medium heat and lightly brush it with vegetable oil. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through.
    3. Toast the Buns: Slice the burger buns in half and toast them lightly on the grill or pan.
    4. Assemble the Burgers: Place a patty on the bottom half of each bun. Add your favorite toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments. Cover with the top half of the bread.
    5. Serve: Enjoy your homemade burgers with fries or a side salad!

Pizza with Pineapple and Tomato

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 ½ cups white wheat flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
For the Toppings:
  • ½ cup tomato sauce (fresh or canned)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • ½ cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 medium tomato (sliced thinly)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Olive oil for drizzling
Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy. In a large mixing bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic (about 8-10 minutes). Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise for about 1 hour in a warm place, until it doubles in size.
  2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F). Place a pizza stone or baking tray inside to heat.
  3. Prepare the Toppings: Slice the tomato thinly and prepare the pineapple chunks. Set them aside. If using fresh tomato sauce, season it with a little salt, pepper, and olive oil for extra flavor.
  4. Assemble the Pizza: Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to parchment paper. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Arrange the pineapple chunks and tomato slices on top. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  5. Bake the Pizza: Carefully transfer the pizza (on parchment paper) to the preheated pizza stone or tray in the oven. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired. Slice and enjoy your delicious homemade Italian pizza with pineapple and tomato!
