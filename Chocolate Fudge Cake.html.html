Ingredients
  • Ground Beef: 500 grams (preferably with some fat for juiciness)
  • Onion: 1 small, finely chopped
  • Egg: 1
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Burger bread
  • Vegetable Oil: 1 tablespoon
  • Optional Toppings: Lettuce, tomato slices, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise


    • Instructions
    1. Prepare the Patties: In a bowl, mix the ground beef, chopped onion, egg, salt, and pepper. Divide the mixture into four equal portions and shape them into patties about 2 cm thick.
    2. Cook the Patties: Heat a grill or frying pan over medium heat and lightly brush it with vegetable oil. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through.
    3. Toast the Buns: Slice the burger buns in half and toast them lightly on the grill or pan.
    4. Assemble the Burgers: Place a patty on the bottom half of each bun. Add your favorite toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments. Cover with the top half of the bread.
    5. Serve: Enjoy your homemade burgers with fries or a side salad!
