Pizza with Pineapple and Tomato

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 ¬Ω cups white wheat flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp dry yeast
  • ¬æ cup warm water
For the Toppings:
  • ¬Ω cup tomato sauce (fresh or canned)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • ¬Ω cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • 1 medium tomato (sliced thinly)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Olive oil for drizzling
Instructions:

Prepare the Dough:

In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy. In a large mixing bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic (about 8-10 minutes). Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise for about 1 hour in a warm place, until it doubles in size.

Preheat the Oven:

Preheat your oven to 220¬∞C (425¬∞F). Place a pizza stone or baking tray inside to heat.

Prepare the Toppings:

Slice the tomato thinly and prepare the pineapple chunks. Set them aside. If using fresh tomato sauce, season it with a little salt, pepper, and olive oil for extra flavor.

Assemble the Pizza:

Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to parchment paper. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Arrange the pineapple chunks and tomato slices on top. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.

Bake the Pizza:

Carefully transfer the pizza (on parchment paper) to the preheated pizza stone or tray in the oven. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Garnish and Serve:

Remove the pizza from the oven. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired. Slice and enjoy your delicious homemade Italian pizza with pineapple and tomato!
