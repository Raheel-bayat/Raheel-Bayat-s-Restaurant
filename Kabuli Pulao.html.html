Ingredients
  • Rice: 2 cups, soaked for 30 minutes
  • Meat: 500 grams
  • Onions: 2 large, thinly sliced
  • Carrots: 2
  • Raisins: 1/2 cup
  • Almonds and Pistachios: 1/4 cup each, slivered
  • Spices: Cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and black pepper
  • Salt: To taste
  • Sugar: 1 tablespoon
  • Oil or Ghee: For cooking
  • Saffron Milk: Optional, for aroma and color
Instructions
  1. Cook the Meat: In a pot, heat oil or ghee and sauté onions until golden brown. Add the meat, salt, and spices. Cook until the meat is tender. Remove the meat and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Rice: Boil water with salt and whole spices (cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves). Cook the soaked rice until 70% done, then drain.
  3. Make Carrots and Raisins: In the same pot, add oil and sauté julienned carrots with sugar until caramelized. Add raisins and cook until plump. Remove and set aside.
  4. Layering: In a large pot, layer the rice, cooked meat, and the carrot-raisin mixture. Sprinkle slivered almonds and pistachios between layers.
  5. Dum Cooking: Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve: Garnish with additional nuts and serve hot with yogurt or salad.
